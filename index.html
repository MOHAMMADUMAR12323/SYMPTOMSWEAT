Is sweating a Covid-19 symptom?

Click the arrow

A doctor in the United Kingdom has revealed a new COVID-19 symptom from the omicron variant — really bad night sweats. Dr. Amir Khan, a physician with the U.K.’s National Health Service, told the U.K. newspaper The Sun that the night sweats are “those kind of drenching night sweats where you might have to get up and change your clothes.” Khan said scratchy throats, dry cough, extreme fatigue and muscle aches are all leading COVID-19 symptoms, too.
